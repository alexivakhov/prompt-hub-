# Privacy Policy for Prompt Hub

Last updated: March 20, 2026

## Introduction
Prompt Hub is a browser extension designed to help you manage your AI prompts. We value your privacy and aim to be transparent about how we handle data.

## Data Collection
Prompt Hub **does not collect, store, or transmit** any of your personal data to external servers. 

## How Data is Handled
- **Local Storage:** Your prompts and categories are stored locally on your device using the `chrome.storage` API.
- **Syncing:** If you are signed into your browser, your data may be synchronized across your devices via Google's secure synchronization service. This data is handled by Google and is not accessible to the developers of Prompt Hub.
- **No Third Parties:** We do not share, sell, or trade your data with any third parties.

## Permissions
- `storage`: Used to save and sync your prompt library.
- `unlimitedStorage`: Used to ensure your library can grow without size restrictions.

## Contact
If you have any questions, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository: https://github.com/alexivakhov/neumorphic-prompt-hub
